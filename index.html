Once a mudbrick waystation along desert trading routes, Riyadh is now one of the wealthiest cities in the world.The country's political,financial and administrative capital is also a city a fascinating subtext.

History

Riyadh is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia. it is also the capital of Riyadh Province, and belongs to the historical regions of Najd and Al-Yahmama

The city is divided into 15 municipal districts, managed by Riyadh Municipality headed by the mayor of Riyadh,and the Riyadah Development Authority,chaired by the govenor of Riyah Province, Khalid bin Bandaar Al Saud
